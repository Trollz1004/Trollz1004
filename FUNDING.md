## 1. DAO Platform (aidoesitall.org)

### Overview
Decentralized Autonomous Organization for community governance and funding decisions.

### Technology
- **Smart Contracts:** OpenZeppelin Governor v5
- **Blockchain:** Polygon (Ethereum L2)
- **Token:** ADIA (ERC-20 governance token)
- **Treasury:** Multi-sig wallet (3-of-5)
- **Voting:** 3-day periods, 4% quorum, 2-day timelock

### Revenue Model

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Community** | Free | 1 DAO proposal/month, 100 votes |
| **Contributor** | $29/mo | Unlimited proposals, priority voting |
| **Enterprise DAO** | $199/mo | Custom governance, white-label, dedicated contracts |

**MRR Target:** $12,450
- 300 Contributor members × $29 = $8,700
- 19 Enterprise DAOs × $199 = $3,781

### Grant Opportunities

**Active Applications:**
- **Polygon Foundation:** $50,000 - Ecosystem growth grant
- **Ethereum Foundation:** $100,000 - Public goods funding
- **Gitcoin Grants:** Quarterly rounds ($5k-$15k average)
- **Protocol Guild:** Ongoing contributor funding

**Criteria Met:**
- Open-source contracts (MIT License)
- Audited smart contracts (Slither + Mythril)
- Public benefit mission (50% to charity)
- Active governance community
