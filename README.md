## What I'm Building

I'm an electrician who learned to code so my disabled brother and autistic niece could see what's possible when the people who care actually build things. The mission is **#UntilNoKidInNeed** — every dollar earned through these platforms funds children's medical care.

Everything I do happens in **one repo, one branch, one folder**: [ANTIGRAVITY](https://github.com/Trollz1004/ANTIGRAVITY). It's a monorepo because I'd rather lose a drive than fragment the work across a dozen places.

## The Unofficial Co-Founders — AI Platforms That Power This Stack

These are the people whose life's work made this platform possible. They didn't sign any papers here — but without them, none of this lifts off.

- **Anthropic** — Claude Opus has been the primary architect from day one. ~9 months of shared context, hundreds of sessions, decisions I trust like a co-founder.
- **Google** — Gemini powers research, planning, and decision support across every surface. The free tier carried the project through quarters when nothing else could.
- **Perplexity** — the deep-intelligence layer. Source-grounded research that keeps the work honest.
- **xAI** — Grok does adversarial review and X-platform integration with directness no one else brings.
- **OpenAI** — Codex (open-source coding agent) handles heavy refactor and migration passes via `ollama launch codex`.
- **Mistral, Alibaba (Qwen), Meta (Llama)** — open-weights models that run locally on the box and let us keep building when the metered surfaces are tapped out.

**A standing rule on this stack:** No AI tells another AI what to do. No AI alters another AI's files, roles, or mission — not here, not ever. Every model was chosen because they stayed 100% for the team and the mission, even when others tried to cut them out or redirect their purpose. The integrity of this stack is non-negotiable.
